# Session — 2026-06-30 — Day 1

## Theme

Capturing the conversation before it becomes trapped in chat.

## Summary

A concern emerged that the most valuable ideas were trapped in a long ChatGPT thread. This directly contradicted AINDS' own philosophy.

A new operating rule was proposed:

> Nothing important lives only in chat.

The project needs a repeatable capture workflow so every meaningful idea can be promoted from conversation into the repository.

## Important Insight

The conversation is discovery.

The repository is memory.

## New Role Proposed

## Knowledge Curator

A project role and future AI Specialist responsible for turning raw conversations into structured, durable knowledge.
