# AINDS Use Cases

These are the real-world problems AINDS is designed to solve.

## Preserve tribal knowledge
Capture the experience of senior designers and engineers so it becomes reusable organizational knowledge.

## Onboard new team members faster
Help new contributors understand not only what the design system contains, but why decisions were made.

## Give AI organizational context
Enable AI to generate work that reflects your organization's standards instead of generic best practices.

## Reduce UX inconsistency
Document interaction patterns so similar problems are solved consistently across products.

## Improve accessibility
Bake accessibility knowledge into the system so it becomes the default rather than an afterthought.

## Scale design systems
Help teams evolve a design system without losing consistency as products and contributors grow.

## Govern component evolution
Provide clear rules for when to reuse, extend, or create new components.

## Make Storybook a knowledge surface
Teach humans and AI not only how components work, but when and why they should be used.

## Accelerate product delivery
Allow teams to spend less time rediscovering decisions and more time building products.

## Create AI Specialists
Provide knowledge that specialized AI agents can consume to perform design, engineering, accessibility, documentation, and governance tasks.

## Future-proof organizational knowledge
Ensure knowledge survives personnel changes, framework migrations, and evolving AI capabilities.

## The Vision
AINDS teaches humans and AI how your organization builds software—not just what to build.
